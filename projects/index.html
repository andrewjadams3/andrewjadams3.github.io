Slateless

Slateless is a simple whiteboard app for teachers and students. It allows teachers to share live drawings and notes with students through a web browser. It also provides a collaborative drawing platform between all users. Slateless was designed for use on tablets, but performs well on almost any platform or device.

Created with Ruby 2.0, Sinatra, PostgreSQL, Foundation, Faye Websockets, Paper.js, and jQuery. Hosted on Heroku.

Slateless is currently a work-in-progress, but feel free to check out the demonstration and source code.
