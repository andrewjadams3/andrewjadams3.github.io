About me

Designer & Developer

I specialize in digital product design and building complex web applications. I enjoy taking an idea from a concept to release, and my desire to contribute to the entire process led me to pursue design in addition to cultivating my skills as a developer.

My career as a product designer officially began at the onset of 2018, but I'd been contributing to the design of Narrative Science products since 2016. As a hybrid developer/designer, I started by wireframing, testing, and ultimately building out new app features for our business intelligence team.

Today I'm involved in all parts of the creative process - from user research and data analysis to prototypes, testing, and mockups. I love the challenge of creating effortless user experience and designing delightful digital products.
